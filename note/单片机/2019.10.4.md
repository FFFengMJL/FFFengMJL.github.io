1. Has CPU
2. Is app-specific
3. Has no OS
4. Real-time(Automotive) 开机后自动死循环
5. Integrated in device
6. Memory constrained
7. Peripheral 外设多
8. Low power
9. Small size
10. Mobile/Network 通讯功能 对讲系统：晶振，电源，储存器，下载电路，复位电路（重启）
11. Cost constrained
